About me

I'm a PhD student at the University of California, Santa Cruz in the Earth and Planetary Sciences department. I recently completed my undergraduate degree in Applied Mathematics at the University of California, Berkeley. My undergrad research focused on modeling the internal structure of the icy moons of Jupiter and Saturn, which are thought to harbor subsurface liquid water oceans.
